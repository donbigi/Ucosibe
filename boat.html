Background Info

I assumed I work as a data analyst for a yacht and boat sales website. The marketing team is preparing a weekly newsletter for boat owners. The newsletter is designed to help sellers to get more views of their boat, as well as stay on top of market trends.

They want me to look at the recent data and help them learn more about the characteristics of the most viewed boat listings in the last 7 days - is it the most expensive boats that get the most views? Are there common features among the most viewed boats?

Data used for the project was provided by Datacamp download here

The dataset columns details

Data Validation

Python programming language was used to clean and manipulate the dataset Click here to view the complete cleaning and manipulation process

The ‘Price’ column contains both the currency and the numeric price. I split the column into two for easy calculations, separating the currency and price.

Splitting the price column enabled me to find all distinct currency.

There are four distinct currencies namely Swiss Franc (CHF), Euro (EUR), Danish Krone (DKK) and British Pound (Â£).

Since there is more than one currency, I converted the currencies to USD to have uniform value for the boat price.

The date of purchase was not listed, so exchange rate used for the conversion was gotten from google currency converter and is valid for January 5, 2022. CHF rate = 1.09, EUR rate = 1.13, DKK rate = 0.15, Pound rate = 1.36.

The size of the boats was not provided,the “length” and “width” columns were used find the size of the boats (Area = length x width).

 

The Objective

  • What are the most viewed boats?
  • is it the most expensive boats that get the most views?
  • What are the common features among the most viewed boats?

In-depth Analysis

Note: the cost of the boats was converted to USD in order to have uniform price for all the boat listing.

Checking the top 20 boats by views, motor yacht tops the list as the most viewed boat.

Looking specifically into the views count of the Motor Yachht showed people are more interested in the used boat market.

People are more interested in the used boat market because it is relatively cheaper than the new counterpart.

Insight from the data shows that a higher price does not guarantee more views.

Customers visiting the site are more interested in modern boats (boats produced post-2000s).

 

Conclusion

The most viewed boat is the “Motor Yacht.” It had significantly more views than the second (about 100,000 more views). Looking deeper into the “Motor Yacht” boat, most of its views come from the used boat market because on average used boats are significantly cheaper than the new counterpart (about 45% less on average).

There is no positive correlation between the cost of the boat and views count. Meaning that a boat being expensive is no guarantee customers will view it.

Some of the features noticeable in the popular boats includes

  • Listing used boats. Customers are more likely to view the used boat section than the new because of the relatively affordable price.
  • Boats built in the 2000s (modern boats) are more likely to be viewed since they are newer (even if they are a used model).

Recommendations

  • The used market is flourishing, capitalize on it to push more sales
  • Encourage listing of modern boats, they are more likely to be viewed
